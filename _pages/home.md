Welcome! I am an Associate Professor in Collaborative Robotics at the Department of Mechanical Engineering,
Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e), The Netherlands.

My research interests include:

- Collaborative and human-centered robots
- Safe motion planning
- Knowledge representation and reasoning for robotic systems
  
I supervise MSc and PhD students and enjoy bridging fundamental robotics research with
real-world applications.
