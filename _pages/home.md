I am **Elena Torta**, Associate Professor in Robotics at the
[Department of Mechanical Engineering, Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e)](https://www.tue.nl).

My research focuses on collaborative robots, where collaboration is the act of two or multiple agents to share skills, knowledge and resources to achieve a common goal.


On this site you can find information about my research, teaching, publications, projects and master theses opportunities.
